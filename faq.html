Frequently Asked Questions

Booking an Appointment

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment here, use our app, by shortcode(*714*33*34#) or call us on 0542053335 / 0202660061.

How soon can I be attended to by a doctor ?

Our doctors will attend to you in realtime using our digital platforms. For physical consultation, our doctors will attend to you within 24 hours.

Can I get Emergency Treatment?

Currently, we do not attend to emergency conditions. If you require emergency treatment, please call 193 or contact your local Accident and Emergency center.

How are my medical Records stored?

Your medical records are stored in a secured and confidential electronic health management system.

What electronic device do I need to book an appointment?

All you need is at least a mobile phone, tablet or computer system.

Can ExpressMed treat children?

Yes, our qualified and professional paediatricians treats children below 18 years under the supervision of their parent or guardian.

Where and when can I request ExpressMed service?

Through our digital platforms you can have access to our services regardless of your location. Home visits are currently available only in Accra with appointment. Register Now and be the first to know when ExpressMed comes to your area.

Payment and Cancellation

How do I pay?

Services are paid for once an appointment is booked or through subscription over a period.

How much does ExpressMed cost?

There are different prices for our various services. Get in touch with us and access all the information you need.

Does ExpressMed accept insurance?

As an out-of-network provider, ExpressMed does not currently accept insurance, however, we are working on providing a solution to this

What happens if I need to cancel my appointment?

If you cancel a visit less than 3 minutes after a doctor is dispatched to your location or digitally assigned to you, you will not be charged anything. If you cancel more than 5 minutes after the doctor is dispatched, you will be charged 50% of the appointment fee.

Will insurance cover my medical expenses ?

We currently do not accept insurance. We are working on an insurance cover plan for our clients. Be the first to know about our insurance plan, subscribe now

During Your Appointment

How long are the appointments?

We offer appointment lengths to suit you. Your paid consultation will be up to a maximum of 2 hours. You can, of course, take more time with the doctor or even ask the doctor to see another member of your family/friends. Extra time or patients will incure additional charges. However, your doctor will notify you of these beforehand

Where does the visit take place?

Our professional doctors will see you at your convenience; on your mobile device or in the comfort of your home or office. Some privacy is necessary when considering an appointment.

If I book an appointment with ExpressMed, who will be treating me?

ExpressMed doctors are registered with the Ghana Medical and Dental council, certified adult and family practitioners with years of experience in their fields. Each of our professional medical team undergoes a rigorous screening process which includes a background check and an interview with our clinical leads. Our clinical team carefully supervises the level of care provided by practitioners and ask for satisfaction from clients.

What if I need a procedure, blood tests labs or x-rays?

Ours doctors will come to your home with everything you need for a thorough visit. Basic labs can be done for you at home too. If you require additional procedures or x-rays that cannot be done in the home, ExpressMed will refer you to the nearest Medical Centre which can best meet your needs.

After Your Appointment

What about prescription?

We will provide private prescriptions. These can be dispensed at any pharmacy or you can use our medication delivery service to receive your prescription within 2 hours.

Do the doctors carry prescription drugs?

ExpressMed doctors do not carry, prescribe or dispense narcotic pain medications or other controlled substances.

Do ExpressMed doctors make referrals?

Your care may require some additional services or treatment outside the customary house call. Your doctor will notify you of any recommended additional services at the time of your visit. Referrals for additional care from other health care providers or institutions are at the discretion of your doctor.

How do I give feedback after my consultation?

After your consultation is completed, ExpressMed will contact you through telephone call, email, or the app will prompt you to provide feedback and rate your doctor. We are continually aiming to provide high levels of patient satisfaction. We would be grateful if you could take the time to tell us your experience after your appointment.

Will others be informed about my consultation?

Your consultation will remain confidential, unless you request a copy of the notes from the visit to pass on to your general practitioners.
