Patrick Annan-Noonoo
CEO
Patrick is a visionary leader with a keen interest in leveraging technology to address social challenges. He has over 8 years’ experience working in the health sector. He is constantly engaged in solving key health issues across Africa; notably HIV/AIDS sensitization in Cote d’Ivoire and Malaria/Cholera prevention in Nigeria, Teenage Pregnancy in Ghana. Patrick is a fellow at Chartered Institute of Marketing, UK; holds an Executive MBA in Project Management at the University of Ghana and Bachelors in Business Management at the University of Cape Coast. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Information Systems. He is the Health Management Consultant at SelStark Global, former Director of External Relations for AIESEC Alumni Ghana and former Curator of Global Shapers Community, Cape Coast Hub, an initiative of World Economic Forum.
