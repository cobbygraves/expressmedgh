Aaron Adom Nathan
CTO
Aaron is a solutions architect who is focused on building solutions to make his community a better place for everyone. He has several years of experience in the banking sector. Aaron is a problem solver. He was the lead developer on the team that developed the ‘Anansi’ web browser and the ‘Anansi’ operating system. Mr. Nathan was also one of those who emerged winners of the Advanced Information Technology Institute (AITI) Innovation challenge in 2013. This was organized in partnership with the Kofi Annan Center of Excellence in ICT. He won with an application named ‘Wekare’, which he intends to apply the knowledge and experience in the Artificial Intelligence field, to help solve health problems in Ghana and Africa at large. He also developed an examination decision support system for the Methodist University College, Ghana in 2014. This application has helped and simplified the examination processes of the university. Mr. Nathan also has international exposure. He developed several applications for international Organizations such as Ecobank Development Corporation in Cameroon and others around the world.
