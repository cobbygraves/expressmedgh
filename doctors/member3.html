Dr. Martin Turkson
CMO
Dr. Martin Mfrase Turkson is a young vivacious medical doctor who has a deep passion for preventive medicine and adolescent health. He currently holds an MSc Medical Sciences and a Bachelor of Medicine & Surgery (MBChB) degree from the University of Ghana School of Medicine and Dentistry. He has vast experience in the field of Family Medicine and is well versed in an analytical and evidence-based approach to healthcare. He aspires to be a Consultant in Family Medicine with an emphasis on Sports and Exercise Medicine and looks forward to a paradigm of universal healthcare coverage in Ghana and across Africa
